OVERVIEW: Describes a given mutation operator.

USAGE: muter operator <operator>

ARGUMENTS:
  <operator>                  Avaiable operators are:
                          RelationalOperatorReplacement, RemoveSideEffects,
                          ChangeLogicalConnector, SwapTernary
                              For all operators use: all

OPTIONS:
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
